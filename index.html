What We Do⬇
GEU delivers high-energy, 3-5 day learning experiences with a specific focus on delivering for adolescent girls in poverty.
Each GEU workshop is unique: designed for specific participants with a specific purpose.
Following the workshop, participants are linked to a global community of GEU alumni, coaches, and experts.
